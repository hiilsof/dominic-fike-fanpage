Awards & Nominations

Nominated for NME Award for Best New Act in the World (2020)

Source: NME Magazine

Dominic Fike was nominated for Best New Act in the World (2020) along side Fontaines DC, DaBaby, Girl In Red, Sam Fender, D-Block Europe, Easy Life, Celeste and Jade Bird but unfortunately lost to Clairo for an event in London on the evening of February 12.

Funny, the event was held in the UK, even though Dominic Fike is banned from touring the UK due to his criminal record. He had this to say:

“It's not forever, We're working on it – sooner or later, I promise.


Nominated for iHeartRadio Music Award for Best New Rock/Alternative Rock Artist

Source: iHeartRadio

Not much is known about the event outside of the live broadcast. Fike unfortunately did not win. Other nominees include The Glorious Sons, Matt Maeson, Dirty Honey and the winner SHAED.
