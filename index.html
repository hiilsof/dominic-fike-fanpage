Dominic Fike Featuring in Euphoria Season 2 & New Album Release in 2022


Source: Variety Magazine. Photo by Daniel Prakopcyk

Dominic Fike lands a main role in the widely popular HBO show "Euphoria" as he describes a "homie" who hangs out with Rue (Zendaya) and Jules (Hunter Schafer). He describes the experience as essentially a “long music video” and get along well with his co-stars describing them as "the nicest people ever". “I don’t have to do much acting,”. “He’s [the character] exactly like me. It’s shit I would say and shit I would do.”

Dominic Fike is also currently working on a new album release that will feature more rapping that will be less "overproduced" than his previous record "What Could Possibly Go Wrong". “I felt like I had too many producers in the room and too many people telling me what was right and what was wrong,” “Now it’s just my mind, similar to how I made ‘Don’t Forget About Me, Demos.’ There were no other people in the room to tell me anything, and I’m not sending this music to other people to give me fucking opinions on it.”

Dominic Fike Rumored to Collab with Kehlani


Source: NME Magazine.

Dominic Fike: “My music is crazy – it blends, it’s weird and it’s all different types of shit”

When asked by a fan on whether he was planning on "dropping" something together with Kehlani, he took to twitter to reply with the handshake and soap emojis. Shortly after he teased upcoming music to his Youtube channel with the caption "What's Next?" on his social media.

As of now, there has been no response from Kehlani and all news covering this story is mere speculation. Most likey the promos on his social media are for his current project that will feature more rapping and less involvement of other music producers.
